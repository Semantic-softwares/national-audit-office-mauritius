Our Services

NAO Audit Services & Departments

We deliver a comprehensive range of audit services to promote accountability, transparency, and good governance across the public sector.

01

Financial Audit

Examination of financial statements and accounts of public entities to ensure accuracy and compliance.

02

Performance Audit

In-depth review of government programmes to assess economy, efficiency, and effectiveness of public spending.

03

Compliance Audit

Verification that public entities adhere to applicable laws, regulations, and financial instructions.

04

IT Audit

Assessment of information systems, controls, and cybersecurity practices in government organisations.

05

Special Investigations

Targeted investigations into suspected financial irregularities or fraud in the public sector.

06

State Enterprise Audit

Review of parastatal bodies and state-owned enterprises to ensure sound corporate governance.

07

Environmental Audit

Audit of environmental policies and initiatives to support sustainable development objectives.

08

Capacity Building

Training and guidance programmes for public sector accountants and finance officers.
