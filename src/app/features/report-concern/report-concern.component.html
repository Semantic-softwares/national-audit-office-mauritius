Report a Concern

If you have information about the misuse of public funds, procurement irregularities or other financial misconduct in a government entity, please report it to the National Audit Office. All reports are treated with the strictest confidentiality.

Your identity is kept confidential
Anonymous reporting available
Protected under applicable law

What to Report

  • Misappropriation or misuse of public funds
  • Irregularities in procurement processes
  • Payroll fraud or ghost employees
  • Bribery or corrupt practices
  • Wasteful or extravagant government spending
  • Failure to collect public revenue
  • Abuse of public property or assets

What Happens Next?

  1. 1 Your report is received and assigned a reference number.
  2. 2 A preliminary assessment is conducted within 10 working days.
  3. 3 Where warranted, the matter is included in the audit programme.
  4. 4 Findings are reported to the National Assembly via the Audit Report.

The NAO is not an investigative body. Information received is used to direct audit work. For criminal matters, please also contact the Independent Commission Against Corruption (ICAC) or the Mauritius Police Force.
